Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to some common questions about our furniture store.

General Questions

What is your return policy?

Our return policy allows you to return most furniture items within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Please ensure that the items are in original condition and packaging.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery services for all our furniture. Delivery charges may vary based on your location and the size of the items purchased.

Are your furniture items customizable?

Some of our furniture pieces are customizable. Please check the product details or contact us for more information on customization options.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order through our website by visiting the "Order Tracking" page. You will also receive tracking information via email once your order is shipped.

Do you offer discounts or promotions?

Yes, we offer periodic discounts and promotions. Be sure to subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on our latest offers and sales.
